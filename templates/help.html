Help & Tutorial

Everything you need to get started with SignAI — tips, shortcuts, and guidance.

Getting Started

1
Create your account

Sign up with your name and email. It's free, no credit card required. Your progress, history, and achievements are saved automatically.

2
Allow camera access

Navigate to the Recognize page and click "Open Camera". The AI reads frames on the server — no video data is stored.

3
Position your hand

Hold your hand 1–2 feet from the camera, palm facing forward. Keep your hand within the frame and ensure good lighting.

4
Sign letters and build words

Hold each sign steady for ~1 second. Confirmed letters are added to the word builder automatically. Use Space to add a space and backspace to delete.

5
Explore learning tools

Use the Dictionary to study each letter, Flashcards to memorize them, and Practice Mode to test your skills with a scored game.

Camera Tips for Best Accuracy

💡
Good lighting

Use a lamp or face a window. Avoid bright backlighting — it silhouettes your hand and reduces accuracy.

✋
Palm facing camera

For most letters, your palm should face the camera directly. Keep your hand upright and steady.

📏
Correct distance

Keep your hand 30–60 cm (1–2 feet) from the camera. Too close or too far reduces landmark detection.

🎨
Plain background

A plain, contrasting background behind your hand helps MediaPipe detect your hand landmarks accurately.

⏱️
Hold steady

Hold each sign for at least 1 second. The AI needs 3–5 consistent frames before confirming a letter.

🔄
Motion letters

Letters J and Z require movement. Trace the shape clearly and steadily — slower movements work better.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is the AI not recognizing my hand?
Check that your hand is in the frame, well-lit, and at an appropriate distance (1–2 feet). Make sure your palm faces the camera. If MediaPipe can't detect landmarks, no prediction will be made — the "No hand" indicator will show.
Is my video or camera feed stored anywhere?
No. SignAI uses server-side OpenCV to capture frames, run MediaPipe hand landmark detection, and classify the pose. Only the predicted letter and confidence score are stored — never raw video or images.
How accurate is the recognition?
Under good lighting conditions, the model achieves 80–95% accuracy on static letters. Motion letters (J, Z) are harder to classify. Confidence scores below 75% are filtered out to reduce false positives.
Why does the app work without a database?
SignAI can run in "demo mode" without MySQL. In this mode, recognition, the dictionary, flashcards, and practice mode all work — but your history, achievements, and progress data won't be saved between sessions.
Can I use SignAI on mobile?
The web interface is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. However, since recognition uses a server-side camera (OpenCV), a physical webcam connected to the server is required — the mobile camera itself cannot be used directly.
How do I reset my progress or delete my account?
Go to Settings to clear your prediction history or delete your account entirely. These actions are permanent and cannot be undone.
