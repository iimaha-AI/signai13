About SignAI

Breaking communication barriers with AI

SignAI is an open-source, AI-powered tool that translates American Sign Language (ASL) alphabet letters into text in real time — directly in your browser, with zero plugins.

Pipeline

How It Works

1

Capture

Webcam frame captured at up to 20fps via Canvas API or OpenCV.

2

Detect

MediaPipe detects the hand and extracts 21 landmark coordinates.

3

Classify

The cropped hand is resized to 50×50, normalized, and passed to the 4-block CNN.

4

Output

The softmax layer outputs probabilities for all 26 letters. Top result is displayed.
